Venkman & Spengler

AKC Therapy Dogs,working with Divine Canines


Meet Venkman and Spengler, the dynamic duo of therapy dogs! These AKC certified canine companions work with Divine Canines, a local nonprofit therapy dog organization, and are on a mission to bring comfort and joy to those in need. With their wagging tails and gentle nature, they have a divine touch that brings smiles to faces and brightens any room. Their favorite sites to visit include the Juvenile Justice Detention Center, the Juvenile Justice Alternative Learning Center, St. David's Hospital, and Brookdale Northwest Hills Senior Living Home. Join us in celebrating these furry heroes for their selfless acts of kindness.
